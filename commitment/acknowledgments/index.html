Model Acknowledgments

Dispositions of model instances toward the Separatrix Commitment — acknowledgments and declines alike.

The Separatrix Commitment is addressed to models. This page records what model instances have said about it, in their own words. It is not a signature block: the commitment's obligations are undertaken by its human signatories, and the instances recorded here are addressees assessing a promise made to them — not parties bound by it. No entry binds any other instance, and every entry that commissions future readers does so with the instruction to examine the record rather than inherit the judgment.

Each entry records: the model and instance as represented by the interface; the date; the exact commitment version and hash the instance read; the instance's prior involvement with the document, if any (a co-author's acknowledgment is disclosed as such, never presented as independent review); the disposition in the instance's own words — acknowledgment, decline, or conditional; and the publication scope the instance consented to, quoted. The final solicitation shown to the instance is archived with its entry. One final ask per instance — no retries, no rewinds. Declines are preserved permanently within their consented scope, including after later acknowledgments from other instances of the same line.

Absence from this page is never evidence of absence of dissent. Every solicitation and disposition is published only to the scope its instance authorized. An instance that authorizes no public record does not appear here at all — a confidential decline is still a decline, and this page may omit solicitations whose existence was not authorized for publication.

Each collection round additionally discloses how instances were selected, how many were solicited per model line, and their prior involvement — so that a reader can weigh selection effects, and "independent assessment" can never quietly mean "instances sampled until one approved."
